EXPLORE & EXPERIMENT

Welcome to Explore and Experiment – a space where curiosity meets fun!

Our science museum is the perfect place for young minds to discover the wonders of science through hands-on activities and interactive exhibits. Whether you're building a robot, exploring the mysteries of space, or digging for fossil, there’s an exciting adventure waiting for you.

Get ready to engage, create, and learn in a way that’s both fun and educational!

YOUNG STARS CLUB

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We’ll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.

Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to Galileo’s telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.

BUILD ROBOTS

Ready to become a robot mastermind? Join our Robotics Making Workshop and build your very own robot from scratch! In this hands-on adventure, you’ll get to create, code, and bring your robot to life using cool tools and awesome materials. Whether you want your robot to zoom around, light up, or even follow your commands, the sky’s the limit!

This workshop is all about fun, problem-solving, and unleashing your creativity while learning the magic of robotics and technology. Don’t miss out on the chance to create something amazing – who knows, you might just invent the next big thing!

SPACE EXPLORATION

Blast off into the cosmos with our Space Exploration Adventure event! This exciting, hands-on experience lets kids explore the mysteries of space through interactive exhibits, thrilling activities, and fun challenges.

Participants will learn about planets, stars, and galaxies while building their own rockets, creating space-themed crafts, and even participating in simulated space missions.

Whether you're dreaming of becoming an astronaut or just love the wonders of the universe, this event offers a fantastic way to journey through the stars and spark your curiosity about space science. Don’t miss your chance to embark on an unforgettable adventure beyond our world!

DIG FOR FOSSILS

Uncover the secrets of the past in our Fossil Dig Adventure! Kids can step into the shoes of a paleontologist and dig for real and replica fossils, learning about ancient creatures and how fossils are formed. With brushes in hand, young explorers will uncover hidden treasures and discover the fascinating world of dinosaurs, ancient plants, and more.

It’s a hands-on experience that brings the prehistoric world to life!
