ABOUT US

Welcome to Community Science Museum, where curiosity comes to life and the world of science, technology, and innovation is at your fingertips!

Our mission is to inspire, educate, and engage visitors of all ages by showcasing the wonders of science and its impact on our everyday lives. Whether you're exploring the mysteries of the universe, learning about the natural world, or diving into the latest advancements in technology, our museum offers a dynamic, hands-on experience that brings learning to life.

OUR STORY

Community Science Museum was created with a vision to make science accessible and exciting for everyone. Since then, we’ve become a hub for learning, exploration, and discovery, with a focus on creating an inclusive space where people of all backgrounds and interests can experience the joy of scientific inquiry. Our exhibits, programs, and events are designed to spark curiosity, ignite passion, and empower visitors to think critically about the world around them.

WHAT MAKES US UNIQUE

At Community Science Museum, we believe in the power of hands-on learning. Our interactive exhibits and live demonstrations make science an experience, not just a lesson. From building your own robot to exploring the vastness of space through virtual reality, every corner of our museum invites you to get involved, ask questions, and experiment.

Our dedicated education team works tirelessly to develop programs, workshops, and events that cater to students, teachers, families, and science enthusiasts. Whether you're here for a guided tour, a family science night, or a summer camp program, we ensure every experience is engaging, informative, and tailored to the needs of our visitors.

OUR IMPACT

Over the years, we has become a cornerstone of the local community, welcoming schools, families, tourists, and science enthusiasts from near and far. With our diverse programming, we aim to inspire the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators. Our educational outreach programs bring science to classrooms, after-school programs, and underserved communities, ensuring that the excitement of discovery reaches as many people as possible.

In addition, our museum plays an active role in the global conversation on scientific literacy, collaborating with other institutions, universities, and research centers to host special events, lectures, and exhibitions that highlight the latest scientific breakthroughs.

JOIN US

Whether you are visiting for a day or planning a return trip, we hope Community Science Museum will be a place where you can spark your curiosity, fuel your imagination, and take away a greater appreciation for the world around you. We can’t wait to explore the world of science with you!
