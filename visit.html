ADMISSION

The entrance is free for all! There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

PLAN YOUR VISIT

PARKING:

On-site parking is available for a small fee. Street parking may also be available nearby.

PUBLIC TRANSIT:

The museum is easily accessible via [bus/subway/metro] lines 12,18,19, with stops right near the entrance.

TICKETS

Tickets can be purchased online for faster entry or at the museum’s front desk. We highly recommend purchasing tickets in advance, especially for weekends or during peak times.

GROUP TICEKTS

For groups of 10 or more, we offer discounted admission.

Group tickets must be purchased in advance. To schedule your visit, please contact our Group Sales team at +47 98765432 or contact@csm.com.

ACCESSIBILITY

PARKING

We have designated accessible parking spaces located near the entrance. These spaces are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

WHEELCHAIR ACCESS

The museum has multiple accessible entrances, including ramps and automatic doors, ensuring easy entry for visitors with mobility devices.

FOOD & DRINKS

Exploring the wonders of science can work up an appetite! At Community Science Museum, we offer a variety of dining options to fuel your visit. Whether you're in the mood for a quick snack or a full meal, our on-site Science Café is the perfect place to relax and refuel.

THE SCIENCE CAFE

Located on floor 1, near the entrance, the Science Café offers a welcoming atmosphere with a menu that features fresh, delicious, and sustainably sourced options. Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or enjoying a leisurely lunch, the café is designed to be a fun, comfortable spot for guests of all ages.

MUSEUM SHOP

Bring home the wonders of science and creativity! The Museum Shop is the perfect place to find unique, science-inspired gifts, fun learning tools, and exciting souvenirs that celebrate discovery and innovation. From interactive toys to educational books, you'll find something for curious minds of all ages!
