FOR SCHOOLS &

KINDERGARDENS

At our science museum, we warmly encourage group visits from schools and kindergartens! Bringing a class or group allows young learners to engage more deeply with hands-on exhibits, interactive displays, and exciting demonstrations. These visits foster teamwork, spark curiosity, and help students connect real-world science with their classroom learning.

Our dedicated staff are on hand to guide and enhance the experience, ensuring that every group leaves inspired and with a greater understanding of the world around them. We look forward to welcoming your group for an unforgettable educational adventure!
